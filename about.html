About Us

Incorporated in 2011, Prognoz Technologies is a private limited company and focuses on providing employability skills and enhancing the soft skills and the technical skills of school dropouts to technical graduates and working professionals, through our meticulously design courses, established skill development processes and competent faculties.

The company is ISO 9001:2015 certified and led by highly qualified and experienced team who is passionate about teaching and mentoring. Our skill development process constitutes of uniquely blended training programs of technical and soft skills which transforms a trainee to become employable and to take real life challenges in industries.

Our USP is the validation process through which each trainee is regularly validated against the respective industrial skills and we ensure that after completing the course, each trainee acquires all necessary skills to become employable and be productive from day one.

Our Vision

To become the leader in enhancing the employability skills and providing the industry with targeted and productive manpower.

Our Mission

Helping them to identify their uniqueness and strengths and putting them to best possible use, with a definite sense of purpose.
